# Research Project: AI Sales Agent Market Landscape

## Mission

Map the AI sales/BDR agent market to understand the competitive landscape, identify whitespace opportunities, and inform a startup investment thesis.

## Decision Context

Identify a startup opportunity in the AI sales agent space. This research will inform whether to build, invest, or pass on this market.

## Success Criteria

- Landscape map with 50+ companies across 5+ categories
- Funding data for at least 80% of companies
- Clear identification of underserved segments
- Actionable investment thesis or build recommendation

## Scope

- Categories: AI SDR/BDR agents, email automation, call automation, meeting scheduling, lead scoring, pipeline intelligence
- Geography: Global, with focus on US-based companies
- Stage: Seed through Series C (exclude pre-product and public companies)
- Depth: Deep — funding, team size, key features, pricing model, customers

## Constraints

- Sessions: 3 overnight runs
- Priority: Quality over breadth — better to have 50 deeply researched companies than 100 surface-level
- Budget: Standard API usage

## Methodology

- Breadth-first within each category, then depth pass on top candidates
- Cross-referenced: key claims verified across 2+ sources
- Dead ends: Flag as INACCESSIBLE, move on, retry in next pass

## Tools

Native (WebSearch + WebFetch)
